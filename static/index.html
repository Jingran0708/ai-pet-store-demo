Cookie & Privacy Notice

What we store
We store a single session cookie (hp_session) in your browser. This cookie contains a random ID that lets us remember your conversation if you refresh the page.

What we do NOT store
We do not store your name, email, phone number, or payment details in cookies. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies. We do not share any data with third parties.

How long
The session cookie expires after 2 hours of inactivity, or when you close the browser.

Your rights (PIPEDA)
Under Canadian privacy law, you have the right to know what data we hold about you. This is a demo prototype — no data is retained after the session ends.
